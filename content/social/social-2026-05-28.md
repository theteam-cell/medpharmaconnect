## LinkedIn

If you're a physician pursuing PSLF, July 1, 2026 is a date worth circling on your calendar.

Starting next month, the Department of Education gains new authority to disqualify certain employers from Public Service Loan Forgiveness eligibility — and changes to income-driven repayment plans could push some doctors' monthly payments higher.

Why does this matter for home buyers? Because on a physician mortgage application, your IDR payment is the number lenders use to calculate your debt-to-income ratio. A higher monthly payment can reduce your qualifying loan amount by tens of thousands of dollars.

If you've been using a "wait for PSLF, then buy" strategy, now is a good time to pressure-test that plan with someone who knows the numbers.
