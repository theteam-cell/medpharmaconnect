## Content Strategy Notes

**Posting Schedule:**
- LinkedIn: Mon, Wed, Fri (3x/week)
- Facebook: Mon, Wed, Thu, Fri (4x/week) + Stories and engagement daily
- Instagram: Daily feed/reel + 2-3 story series per day (5x/week content)
- X/Twitter: 5x/week (daily tweets, varies between single tweets, threads, polls)
- YouTube: 1x/week (Wednesday) + weekly Shorts
- TikTok: Mon, Tue, Thu (3x/week) + engagement/responses ongoing

**Content Themes (Rotate):**
1. **Education & How-To** (40% of calendar)
2. **Myth-Busting & Objection Handling** (15%)
